Delete Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here is how you can permanently delete your account and all associated data.

⚠️ Warning

Deleting your account is permanent. All your match history, statistics, and tournament data will be erased and cannot be recovered.

Instructions

  1. Open the Padel Companion app on your iPhone or Android device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
  4. Tap on Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your identity and confirm the deletion.

If you no longer have access to the app, please contact support to request account deletion.
